Camera Control System

Demo Video

As part of Engineers for a Sustainable World amphibious camera trap team, I improved the motion detection system and built a control system. The motion detection system uses a Raspberry Pi with a low-res camera to trigger a high-res DSLR. I built and programmed a system to allow adjustment of the system's settings in the field
